Our Story

Maison de Gâteaux (House of Cakes), opened its doors for the first time on May 25th, 2018. Since then, we have grown far and beyond our dreams could have imagined, and have become a landmark in the city of Dearborn, where we are located.

We offer daily our freshly baked, scratch-made pastries, occasion cakes, macarons, croissants, coffees, teas, and more.

We have catered to the Wayne County area for all kinds of occasions, including baby showers, bridal showers, engagements, weddings, and the like. We offer everything from miniature pastries, custom cakes, to fully decorated dessert tables for all occasions. You can contact us to learn more about how we can make your day special.
